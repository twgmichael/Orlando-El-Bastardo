# ORLANDO EL BASTARDO
## Episode 1: "THE PILOT"

**One-Hour Animated Television Pilot**  
**Approximate Runtime: 50 Minutes**  
**Format: Teleplay / Fountain-Compatible Markdown**

FADE IN:

# TEASER

## 1. EXT. DEEP SPACE

Black.

A field of hard white stars.

A small RED SPACECRAFT tears across frame at absurd speed.

The JOURNEYBLASTER FIVE THOUSAND is unmistakable: compact saucer hull, transparent bubble canopy, twin exposed engine pods, bright red finish, and just enough dents to suggest its owner does not respect warranties.

A small asteroid spins ahead.

The JourneyBlaster does not turn.

Inside the canopy, ORLANDO EL BASTARDO, late thirties to early forties, unshaven, confident, and dressed like a man who considers regulations a form of light reading, watches the asteroid grow.

He takes a bite from a sandwich.

## 2. INT. JOURNEYBLASTER - COCKPIT

The cockpit is half exploration vehicle, half bachelor apartment.

A reclining pilot’s chair. Oversized beverage holders. Mini-fridge. Microwave. A half-disassembled sensor relay held together with red tape.

The SHIP AI speaks in a calm, pleasant voice.

**SHIP AI**  
Collision probability has risen to ninety-eight point four percent.

Orlando chews.

**ORLANDO**  
That still leaves one point six percent.

**SHIP AI**  
Your interpretation of probability remains unconventional.

The asteroid fills the canopy.

At the last possible second, Orlando taps a control.

The ship rotates ninety degrees, slides through a narrow fracture in the asteroid, and emerges from the other side without slowing.

A sensor mast scrapes rock.

Sparks.

**SHIP AI**  
External sensor array three has been damaged.

**ORLANDO**  
Put it on the list.

**SHIP AI**  
It is already on the list.

Orlando looks at a display.

A primitive MINING PROBE tumbles nearby, trapped against the asteroid by a failed thruster.

Its emergency beacon blinks.

**ORLANDO**  
There you are.

He pulls the JourneyBlaster alongside.

Mechanical clamps extend.

## 3. EXT. ASTEROID FIELD

The JourneyBlaster catches the tumbling probe and stabilizes it.

A second asteroid breaks apart nearby.

A cloud of debris races toward them.

## 4. INT. JOURNEYBLASTER - COCKPIT

**SHIP AI**  
New collision probability: one hundred percent.

**ORLANDO**  
See? Much easier to plan around.

Orlando slams the throttle.

The JourneyBlaster dives, probe still attached.

Debris hammers the ship.

The pilot compartment shakes violently.

Orlando protects his sandwich.

## 5. EXT. ASTEROID FIELD

The red ship breaks free of the debris cloud, towing the probe.

It clears the field and coasts into open space.

## 6. INT. JOURNEYBLASTER - COCKPIT

Orlando checks the probe telemetry.

**ORLANDO**  
Survey package intact. Mining company gets its data. I get paid. Everybody wins.

**SHIP AI**  
External sensor array three does not win.

**ORLANDO**  
Nobody likes a sore loser.

A communications panel flashes.

ENCRYPTED PRIORITY SIGNAL.

Orlando’s expression changes.

He sets down the sandwich.

**ORLANDO**  
Source?

**SHIP AI**  
Union Fleet encryption. Obsolete command cipher. Personal authorization key attached.

The screen resolves into a damaged recording.

COMMANDER CASEY EPSTEIN, forties, sandy blond hair, bruised face, sits somewhere dark. Alarm lights flash behind him.

**CASEY (RECORDING)**  
Orlando. If you get this, I need you.

Static.

**CASEY (RECORDING)**  
Starbase Eighty-Six. Red Dragon Inn. Come alone.

A distant BLAST shakes Casey’s camera.

He looks off-screen.

**CASEY (RECORDING)**  
And for once in your life, do not be late.

The message cuts.

Orlando stares at the empty screen.

**SHIP AI**  
The transmission is eleven hours old.

**ORLANDO**  
Set course for Starbase Eighty-Six.

**SHIP AI**  
Your commercial survey contract remains incomplete.

Orlando releases the mining probe. It coasts safely toward a recovery beacon.

**ORLANDO**  
Bill them for the sensor mast.

He pushes the throttle.

## 7. EXT. DEEP SPACE

The JourneyBlaster accelerates.

Stars stretch into brilliant lines.

The ship vanishes into HYPERSPACE.

SMASH CUT TO:

# TITLE SEQUENCE

A ringed planet forms from a field of stars.

The JourneyBlaster streaks through it.

Bold dimensional lettering assembles:

# ORLANDO EL BASTARDO

The red ship banks past camera.

CUT TO BLACK.

# ACT ONE

## 8. EXT. SETI ORAGAMI SEVEN - SPACE

A dull frontier planet.

STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX hangs above it.

The station is a patched, overcrowded collection of docking cylinders, habitation rings, cargo spines, and additions nobody bothered to align.

Freighters queue around it.

One ship vents atmosphere.

Nobody reacts.

## 9. INT. JOURNEYBLASTER - COCKPIT

The station fills the canopy.

**STATION CONTROL (V.O.)**  
JourneyBlaster N-Two-Zero-One Foxtrot X-Ray, reduce approach velocity and join commercial queue seven.

Orlando scans the traffic line.

It extends for miles.

**ORLANDO**  
Station Control, N-Two-Zero-One Foxtrot X-Ray requests priority docking.

**STATION CONTROL (V.O.)**  
Priority denied.

**ORLANDO**  
Medical emergency.

**STATION CONTROL (V.O.)**  
State the nature of the emergency.

Orlando glances at the clock.

**ORLANDO**  
I am developing impatience.

A beat.

**STATION CONTROL (V.O.)**  
Join queue seven.

The AI highlights a maintenance corridor along the station’s underside.

**SHIP AI**  
Service aperture ninety-three is presently open.

Orlando smiles.

## 10. EXT. STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX

The JourneyBlaster drops out of line, slips beneath a cargo hauler, rolls sideways between two docking pylons, and darts into SERVICE APERTURE 93.

A traffic beacon spins wildly in its wake.

## 11. INT. STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX - HANGAR 93

The JourneyBlaster settles onto repulsor lifts.

Dockworkers stare.

A furious DOCK OFFICIAL hurries toward it.

The canopy opens.

**DOCK OFFICIAL**  
You cannot dock here.

Orlando climbs down.

**ORLANDO**  
Then we agree this should be brief.

He tosses the official a credit card and keeps walking.

**DOCK OFFICIAL**  
This is ten credits.

**ORLANDO**  
Do not spend it all on customer service.

## 12. INT. STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX - COMMERCIAL PROMENADE

Orlando moves through a crowded, dimly lit frontier concourse.

Smugglers. Mercenaries. Crewmen sleeping against cargo crates. A six-armed DERANTI vendor sells imitation Fleet badges beside a noodle stand.

A station announcement crackles overhead.

**STATION ANNOUNCER (V.O.)**  
Life-support interruption in sectors twelve through fifteen has been reclassified as scheduled maintenance.

No one looks up.

Orlando checks Casey’s message on a handheld display.

RED DRAGON INN.

## 13. INT. RED DRAGON INN - WIDE

A frontier bar trying very hard to look respectable.

The attempt failed years ago.

The bar sits in the middle of the room. Behind it, a heavyset BARTENDER polishes a glass.

Patrons occupy smoky booths and shadowed tables.

The entrance opens at frame left.

Orlando enters, crosses left to right, and surveys the room.

No Casey.

He takes a booth with a clear view of both exits.

## 14. INT. RED DRAGON INN - ORLANDO'S BOOTH

A tepid beer sits untouched.

Orlando checks his watch.

Then again.

He studies each new arrival.

Nobody matches Casey.

A WAITRESS passes.

**ORLANDO**  
Has anyone asked for me?

**WAITRESS**  
Who are you?

**ORLANDO**  
That answers the question.

She leaves.

Orlando activates Casey’s recording again, volume low.

**CASEY (RECORDING)**  
And for once in your life, do not be late.

Orlando shuts it off.

He finishes the beer, stands, and crosses to the bar.

## 15. INT. RED DRAGON INN - BAR

The bartender continues cleaning the glass.

Orlando sits on a stool.

The bartender ignores him until Orlando settles.

Then the bartender walks over and faces him.

CUT TO MEDIUM SHOT - BARTENDER.

**BARTENDER**  
What’ll it be?

CUT TO MEDIUM SHOT - ORLANDO FROM BEHIND.

**ORLANDO**  
Strongest you got.

The bartender pours something dark and smoking.

Orlando does not touch it.

**ORLANDO**  
I am looking for a man about my height. Sandy blond hair. Usually looks like somebody just explained arithmetic to him.

The bartender spits onto the artificial mahogany bar and rubs it in with the cloth.

**BARTENDER**  
Buddy, I don’t know nothin’.

Orlando places a fifty-credit card on the counter.

The bartender’s eyes track it.

**ORLANDO**  
Would fifty credits improve your education?

The bartender reaches.

Orlando slides the card back.

**BARTENDER**  
Guy like that was here two days ago.

**ORLANDO**  
More.

**BARTENDER**  
Two men with him.

**ORLANDO**  
More.

The bartender’s face reddens.

**BARTENDER**  
One wore Union Fleet jacks.

**ORLANDO**  
And you have not seen any of them since.

**BARTENDER**  
No.

Orlando considers the card.

**ORLANDO**  
That was not worth fifty.

He adjusts its value.

**ORLANDO**  
Ten.

He tosses it down.

The bartender HOWLS and lunges.

Orlando catches both forearms and yanks.

The bartender comes halfway across the counter, feet kicking behind him.

Orlando pins him there and places one boot beside the bartender’s head.

Conversation around the room stops.

Orlando produces a compact MINI-BLASTER.

**ORLANDO**  
Anything else before your head becomes a serving dish?

**BARTENDER**  
That’s all! I only work part-time!

**ORLANDO**  
That explains the beer.

**BARTENDER**  
I’ll get better beer.

**ORLANDO**  
And beer nuts.

**BARTENDER**  
And beer nuts.

Orlando releases him.

The bartender drops behind the bar.

He starts to rise.

Orlando gives him a sharp kick.

The bartender collapses.

Orlando faces the room.

**ORLANDO**  
Drinks are on the house.

The patrons surge toward the bar.

Orlando steps off the stool and exits the way he came.

## 16. INT. STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX - CORRIDOR

Orlando walks quickly.

His communicator chirps.

**SHIP AI (V.O.)**  
Captain, two unidentified individuals have attempted remote access to the JourneyBlaster.

**ORLANDO**  
Lock out all external controls.

**SHIP AI (V.O.)**  
Already completed.

**ORLANDO**  
See? This relationship works.

A faint GROAN echoes from a service passage.

Orlando stops.

Another groan.

He draws the mini-blaster and approaches.

## 17. INT. STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX - SERVICE PASSAGE

A figure leans against the wall in shadow.

Blood streaks the paneling.

**ORLANDO**  
Show yourself, or choose a floral arrangement.

The figure slides down the wall.

Orlando rushes forward.

CASEY.

Blue work coveralls soaked in blood. Blaster wound in his side.

**ORLANDO**  
Casey.

Casey struggles to focus.

**CASEY**  
You made it.

**ORLANDO**  
You look terrible.

**CASEY**  
You always say that.

Orlando lowers beside him and presses a hand against the wound.

**ORLANDO**  
Medic channel?

**CASEY**  
No time.

He forces a DATA WAFER into Orlando’s hand.

**CASEY**  
Take that to Command. Fleet Intelligence. Admiral Farring.

**ORLANDO**  
You can tell him yourself.

**CASEY**  
No.

Casey grips Orlando’s sleeve.

**CASEY**  
They have codes. Fleet codes. Research access. Colony defense maps.

Orlando’s expression hardens.

**ORLANDO**  
Who?

A distant metallic CLANG.

Casey looks toward the corridor.

**CASEY**  
They followed me.

An EXPLOSION tears apart the far intersection.

Smoke and debris.

Blaster bolts rip through the haze.

**VOICE (O.S.)**  
Give it up, Fleet boy!

Orlando fires blindly.

A scream answers.

Casey snatches Orlando’s mini-blaster.

**ORLANDO**  
What are you doing?

**CASEY**  
Covering your escape.

**ORLANDO**  
You cannot stand.

**CASEY**  
Then they won’t expect me to shoot straight.

He fires into the smoke.

**CASEY**  
Go.

Orlando hesitates.

**CASEY**  
You a tourist now? Go, shithead!

Orlando gives Casey a sharp Fleet salute.

Casey returns it weakly.

Orlando runs toward Hangar 93.

Casey turns to the smoke, blaster braced in both hands.

**CASEY**  
Come on, you ugly bastards.

He opens fire.

## 18. INT. STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX - HANGAR 93

Orlando bursts in.

The JourneyBlaster’s red hull gleams beneath industrial lights.

For the briefest moment, he looks relieved to see it.

The AI detects him.

The canopy turns transparent and opens.

**SHIP AI**  
Good evening, Captain Bastardo. I trust your shore leave was pleasant.

**ORLANDO**  
Emergency launch sequence Baker.

Orlando climbs in.

## 19. INT. JOURNEYBLASTER - COCKPIT

Harness locks.

Canopy seals.

Displays ignite.

**SHIP AI**  
Emergency Launch Baker confirmed.

The hangar entry door shudders under weapons fire.

## 20. INT. STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX - HANGAR CONTROL

A station controller sees the JourneyBlaster powering up.

**STATION CONTROL**  
JourneyBlaster N-Two-Zero-One Foxtrot X-Ray, launch clearance has not been granted.

## 21. INT. JOURNEYBLASTER - COCKPIT

**ORLANDO**  
Station Control, Union Fleet emergency. Captain Orlando El Bastardo. Fleet number Alpha Charlie Echo Zero Zero Seven. Authorize immediate departure.

**STATION CONTROL (V.O.)**  
Your rank is not recognized under Seti Oragami Colonial Government.

The inner hangar door begins to buckle.

**STATION CONTROL (V.O.)**  
Power down pending review.

Orlando arms the twin forward FRAP RAY BLASTERS.

**STATION CONTROL (V.O.)**  
Sensors indicate offensive systems have been activated. Such action violates—

**ORLANDO**  
Doesn’t recognize my rank.

He sights the outer doors.

**ORLANDO**  
Fine.

He fires.

## 22. INT. STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX - HANGAR 93

Blue-white energy pounds the outer doors.

Metal deforms.

The inner door bursts open behind Orlando.

ARMED PURSUERS spill into the hangar.

Orlando fires again.

The outer doors EXPLODE into space.

Atmosphere tears through the opening.

Crates, tools, and pursuers fly toward the breach.

Several men cling desperately to the inner doorway.

The JourneyBlaster rotates on its repulsors.

## 23. INT. JOURNEYBLASTER - COCKPIT

Orlando looks back.

**ORLANDO**  
And now you have a ventilation problem.

He slams the throttle.

# END ACT ONE

# ACT TWO

## 24. EXT. STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX

The JourneyBlaster blasts through the ruined hangar doors.

Debris ricochets off its hull.

Station defense turrets rotate.

Green weapons fire cuts across space.

The red ship dives along the station’s outer ring.

## 25. INT. JOURNEYBLASTER - COCKPIT

Warnings flash.

**SHIP AI**  
Multiple weapons locks.

**ORLANDO**  
Are any of them competent?

A proton torpedo races toward them.

Orlando waits.

Waits.

Then rolls aside.

The torpedo misses and slams into a station antenna array.

**ORLANDO**  
Apparently not.

Two station fighters launch behind him.

## 26. EXT. STARBASE EIGHTY-SIX - CHASE

The JourneyBlaster threads between habitat modules.

Fighters pursue.

Green bolts strike the station around Orlando.

He dives beneath a rotating docking arm.

One fighter follows too closely.

Its wing clips the arm and tears free.

The second fighter fires continuously.

Orlando cuts thrust.

The JourneyBlaster drops backward relative to the fighter.

The fighter overshoots.

Orlando fires one short burst into its engine housing.

The fighter spins away, disabled.

## 27. INT. JOURNEYBLASTER - COCKPIT

A countdown appears.

SAFE HYPERSPACE DISTANCE: 00:18.

**SHIP AI**  
Station batteries are recalibrating.

**ORLANDO**  
Tell them to take their time.

A heavy cannon on the station fires.

The blast narrowly misses and washes the cockpit in light.

**SHIP AI**  
They declined.

Orlando angles away from the station.

SAFE DISTANCE: 00:05.

Another torpedo locks on.

**ORLANDO**  
Come on.

00:03.

The torpedo closes.

00:01.

The navigation display lights.

Orlando pulls the hyperspace throttle.

## 28. EXT. DEEP SPACE

The stars stretch.

The JourneyBlaster vanishes.

The torpedo passes through empty space.

## 29. INT. JOURNEYBLASTER - COCKPIT - HYPERSPACE

Mottled light washes across Orlando’s face.

He stares at Casey’s blood on his hand.

The humor is gone.

**SHIP AI**  
Course established for Starbase McKinley.

Orlando grips the data wafer.

**ORLANDO**  
Full speed.

**SHIP AI**  
The starboard engine is operating above recommended tolerance.

**ORLANDO**  
So am I.

## 30. EXT. STARBASE MCKINLEY - SPACE

A massive Union installation shaped like a broad mushroom with four enormous docking bays.

Union cruisers patrol nearby.

The JourneyBlaster exits hyperspace and approaches.

## 31. INT. STARBASE MCKINLEY - BURGER COUNTER

Orlando sits at a counter with a burger piled high with olives.

He eats quickly.

A Fleet SECURITY OFFICER stands beside him.

**SECURITY OFFICER**  
Admiral Farring has been waiting ninety minutes.

**ORLANDO**  
Then another three minutes will not kill him.

The officer looks at the burger.

**SECURITY OFFICER**  
Are those olives?

**ORLANDO**  
They are what separates civilization from chaos.

## 32. INT. STARBASE MCKINLEY - FLEET INTELLIGENCE RECEPTION

Artificial wood doors. Glass panels etched with the Union crest.

A bright blue, six-armed DERANTI SECRETARY sits behind a desk. One hand types. One sorts files. One paints a nail. Two answer calls.

The sixth is out of sight.

Orlando approaches while looking deliberately at the ceiling.

**ORLANDO**  
Captain Bastardo to see Admiral Farring.

The secretary giggles.

**DERANTI SECRETARY**  
The Admiral has been expecting you.

She giggles again.

Orlando moves quickly to the office door.

## 33. INT. ADMIRAL FARRING'S OFFICE

ADMIRAL FARRING, late fifties, disciplined, controlled, and already exhausted by Orlando, sits behind a clean desk.

Both hands visible.

Orlando notices.

**FARRING**  
Captain, you are late.

**ORLANDO**  
Yes.

Orlando sits without invitation.

Farring slides the data wafer into a reader.

**FARRING**  
You also destroyed a hangar bay, disabled two station fighters, violated local traffic law, and transmitted an obscenity on an open control channel.

**ORLANDO**  
That last one was diplomatic context.

Farring studies him.

**FARRING**  
Commander Epstein is dead?

Orlando’s expression closes.

**ORLANDO**  
He died doing his duty.

Farring nods once.

**FARRING**  
Casey had been investigating unauthorized access to colonial defense networks.

A wall display activates.

A GALACTIC MAP appears.

Union territory in blue. Bugblatter space in red. An unstable border region between them.

**FARRING**  
The Ventradi operate here. Neither side patrols aggressively because neither side wants an incident.

**ORLANDO**  
So the pirates get a free hand.

**FARRING**  
Until recently they used it badly. Small raids. Salvage theft. Extortion.

A mining world flashes.

**FARRING**  
Three weeks ago, Ommpooli Two was attacked. Highly developed mining colony. Defense grid neutralized. Warehouses entered with precise knowledge of the layout.

Images: ruined structures, dead colonists, empty storage bays.

**FARRING**  
The attackers stole one material and nothing else.

A crystal sample appears.

**FARRING**  
An unnamed crystalline by-product with unusual energy properties.

**ORLANDO**  
And Casey found someone accessing the colony defense maps.

**FARRING**  
Among other things.

The display changes to a blurry image of HOROUS, an Antaren altered to appear more human.

**FARRING**  
Fleet Captain Horous. Antaren. Former Academy cadet. Now believed to be consolidating the Ventradi captains.

**ORLANDO**  
He looks ambitious.

**FARRING**  
He is organized. That makes him more dangerous than the average pirate.

**ORLANDO**  
Average pirate being barely qualified to operate a door.

Farring brings up another location.

A classified research outpost hidden in an asteroid.

**FARRING**  
The material from Ommpooli Two was being evaluated here.

**ORLANDO**  
Secret weapons station.

**FARRING**  
You do not know that.

**ORLANDO**  
Then why did you lower your voice?

Farring ignores him.

**FARRING**  
You will deliver Casey’s data and assist the research staff.

**ORLANDO**  
Send Jenkins.

**FARRING**  
I was going to.

Orlando brightens.

**FARRING**  
Then you volunteered.

Orlando’s face falls.

**ORLANDO**  
I did no such thing.

**FARRING**  
You are the best field analyst available.

**ORLANDO**  
That is an indictment of the Service.

Farring stands.

**FARRING**  
Casey trusted you. Find out why he died.

That lands.

Orlando rises.

**ORLANDO**  
I will.

Farring hands him an authorization module.

**FARRING**  
Level Zero access. Do not abuse it.

Orlando pockets it.

**ORLANDO**  
Define abuse.

**FARRING**  
Get out.

## 34. INT. STARBASE MCKINLEY - CORRIDOR

Orlando exits.

The Deranti secretary looks up and giggles.

**DERANTI SECRETARY**  
Did it go well?

**ORLANDO**  
I have been promoted to unpaid trouble.

He walks on.

# END ACT TWO

# ACT THREE

## 35. EXT. CLASSIFIED ASTEROID SYSTEM

An unremarkable brown asteroid orbiting an unremarkable star.

The JourneyBlaster approaches.

A section of rock disappears, revealing a concealed hangar.

A tractor beam captures the red ship.

## 36. INT. RESEARCH OUTPOST - HANGAR

The JourneyBlaster settles.

The canopy opens.

DOCTOR NYE QUILL, forties, tall, thin, articulate, and professionally cordial, approaches.

**QUILL**  
Captain Bastardo. Doctor Nye Quill, chief researcher.

Orlando climbs down.

**ORLANDO**  
You know why I’m here.

**QUILL**  
If we did not, you would not be here.

Orlando gives him a look.

Quill gestures toward a security door.

## 37. INT. RESEARCH OUTPOST - SECURITY CHECKPOINT

Quill places his hand on a scanner.

**COMPUTER**  
Doctor Nye Quill verified. Guest identification required.

Orlando places his hand on the scanner.

**ORLANDO**  
Computer. Orlando El Bastardo. Full Level Zero access.

**COMPUTER**  
Voice and handprint recorded. Verification pending.

**ORLANDO**  
What is the gravitational constant of the universe?

Quill looks at him.

**ORLANDO**  
What is the equation for hyperspace transition? What is the Planck constant? What is the sound of one hand clapping?

The computer pauses.

**COMPUTER**  
The gravitational constant is dependent on frame of reference—

Quill’s eyes widen.

The computer continues with increasingly confused answers.

**COMPUTER**  
The Planck constant is... something about a person and another thing.

The voice abruptly changes to a MALE TECHNICIAN.

**MALE TECHNICIAN (V.O.)**  
Please stand by.

Orlando leans toward the scanner.

**ORLANDO**  
Computer. Verify Orlando El Bastardo. Level Zero.

A beat.

**COMPUTER**  
Orlando El Bastardo. Full Level Zero clearance verified.

Quill stares.

**ORLANDO**  
And get me a cheese sandwich.

**COMPUTER**  
One cheese sandwich.

The door opens.

**QUILL**  
What did you do?

**ORLANDO**  
Old bar trick.

**QUILL**  
You broke through the most sophisticated security network in the Union.

**ORLANDO**  
I have an unfair advantage.

**QUILL**  
Which is?

Orlando enters.

**ORLANDO**  
I’ve slept with programmers.

## 38. INT. RESEARCH OUTPOST - TESTING FACILITY

A vast laboratory.

Energy weapons fire into armored test slabs.

Materials vaporize, burn, and atomically distort.

DR. PENN, thirties, enthusiastic and socially awkward, monitors a firing test.

A large cannon discharges.

A plate of durasteel turns to ash.

Orlando enters eating his replicated cheese sandwich.

**ORLANDO**  
You have burritos?

Penn looks up.

**PENN**  
A what?

**ORLANDO**  
Never mind.

Quill joins them.

**QUILL**  
Doctor Penn, show the Captain the Ommpooli material.

Penn brings up a molecular diagram.

**PENN**  
It is technically not an ore. More accurately a crystalline by-product. High-density quartz impregnated with platinum and silver—

**ORLANDO**  
What does it do?

Penn suppresses irritation.

**PENN**  
When used with phased-energy inducers, it creates power magnification approaching three hundred percent.

Orlando lowers the sandwich.

**ORLANDO**  
That explains how pirates penetrated a developed colony.

**PENN**  
Not exactly. The amplification is unstable. The energy loses coherence.

A test recording plays.

A beam strikes a target.

The impact blooms unpredictably several meters away.

**PENN**  
The effect wanders.

**ORLANDO**  
Energy does not wander.

**PENN**  
This does.

Quill folds his arms.

**QUILL**  
The material is useless as a precision weapon.

**ORLANDO**  
A pirate does not need precision. He needs fear.

Penn calls up sensor records.

**PENN**  
The blast also creates interference.

Orlando steps closer.

**ORLANDO**  
Which sensors?

Penn scrolls.

**PENN**  
Radar disrupted. Mass detection skewed. Life-form readings jammed. Quantum energy detectors flooded. Mid-range electromagnetic detection skewed. Long-range electromagnetic detection disrupted.

Orlando thinks.

**ORLANDO**  
Graviton detection?

**PENN**  
Unaffected.

Orlando turns to Quill.

**ORLANDO**  
It is not a weapon.

Quill sees it.

**QUILL**  
It is a jamming device.

**ORLANDO**  
Wrapped around an explosion.

Penn considers the implications.

**PENN**  
The attacker would be blind as well.

**ORLANDO**  
The attacker moves. Planetary defenses do not.

He points at the test display.

**ORLANDO**  
Fire this into a defense grid, flood every common sensor, triangulate the fixed emplacements by position, and destroy them while they are guessing what happened.

Silence.

Quill looks unsettled.

**QUILL**  
That would make conventional counter-jamming useless.

**ORLANDO**  
Which means whoever designed this understood exactly what colonial defenses depend on.
