                               TEASER

INT. ORBITAL STATION LOUNGE - NIGHT

WIDE SHOT

A well-worn lounge aboard a bustling space station. Warm amber lighting contrasts with the cool glow of instrument panels and viewport reflections. Shelves of bottles line a polished alloy bar. Through distant observation windows, stars drift silently past against the blackness of space. A handful of station personnel quietly occupy tables and booths. The low hum of life support blends with subdued conversation.

Centered behind the bar, the BARTENDER methodically polishes a clean drinking glass with a white towel. Their attention never leaves the task.

The pressure door slides open.

Our HERO enters frame from the LEFT.

The Hero pauses briefly, scanning the lounge without urgency. The Bartender does not acknowledge the arrival and continues cleaning the glass.

The Hero crosses the room from LEFT TO RIGHT, boots clicking softly on the deck plating.

Passing several empty tables, the Hero approaches the bar.

The Bartender remains focused on the glass.

The Hero reaches an empty stool near center frame, pulls it out, and sits.

The stool gives a soft scrape against the deck.

Only then does the Bartender set the glass down.

The Bartender places the towel over one shoulder, turns, and walks calmly toward the Hero.

Stopping directly opposite the Hero, the Bartender rests both hands lightly on the bar.

                                                         CUT TO:

MEDIUM SHOT - BARTENDER

The Bartender studies the Hero for a quiet beat before speaking.

                              BARTENDER
                  What'll it be?

                                                         CUT TO:

MEDIUM SHOT - HERO (FROM BEHIND)

The Hero sits relaxed on the stool, shoulders squared toward the Bartender. The Bartender remains visible beyond.

Without hesitation—

                              HERO
                  Strongest you got.

                                                         CUT TO:

WIDE SHOT

The Hero immediately rises from the stool.

Without another word, the Hero turns and walks from RIGHT TO LEFT, retracing the same path across the lounge.

The Bartender watches silently from behind the bar, saying nothing.

The Hero reaches the pressure door, which slides open automatically, and exits into the station corridor.

The door seals shut.

The Bartender picks up the same glass, resumes polishing it with the towel, and returns to work as though nothing had happened.

                                                           FADE OUT.
